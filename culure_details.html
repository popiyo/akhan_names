Akan Naming Convention

From the Akan Culture, we can now see how the names are given based on the days of the week:

Weekday Male Names Female Names
Sunday Kwasi Akosua
Monday Kwadho Adwoa
Tuesday Kwabena Abenaa
Wednesday Kwaku Akua
Thursday Yaw Yaa
Friday Kofi Afua
Saturday Kwame Ama
